Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a beloved Italian comfort dish layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey melted cheese. Perfect for family gatherings or a cozy night in, this hearty meal is satisfying and flavorful, bringing together a blend of textures in every bite. This classic recipe creates a balanced lasagna with tender pasta, savory beef, and a hint of herbs that truly make it a crowd-pleaser.

Ingridients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheeser
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg
  • cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • herbs and spices to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook Lasagna Noodles: Boil noodles according to package instructions, then drain and lay them flat on a clean surface.
  3. Prepare Meat Sauce: In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add diced onion and garlic, cooking until softened. Stir in marinara sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix Ricotta Layer: In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and a bit of salt.
  5. Assemble Lasagna: In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Place three noodles on top, followed by ⅓ of the ricotta mixture, ⅓ of the meat sauce, and ⅓ of the mozzarella and Parmesan. Repeat layers two more times.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden
  7. Serve: Let rest for 10 minutes before cutting. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
